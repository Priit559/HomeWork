On The Road: A Fixed Gear Journey

March 2, 2025

The Pure Joy of Fixed Gear Riding

The Beginning

Five years ago, I discovered the raw simplicity of fixed gear cycling. One gear, no freewheel, just pure connection between rider and machine. The streets of our city became my playground, each pedal stroke a deliberate dance with gravity.

"In the world of fixed gear cycling, every rotation tells a story, every skid marks a memory."

The Community

What started as a solitary pursuit quickly evolved into something bigger. Every Thursday night, our growing community of fixed gear enthusiasts gather for urban rides. From seasoned messengers to curious newcomers, we share one common bond: the love of riding fixed.

The Philosophy

Fixed gear riding isn't just about transportation; it's a philosophy. It teaches you to read the road, anticipate challenges, and become one with your bicycle. There's no coasting through life when you're riding fixed – you're always engaged, always present.

Essential Fixed Gear Tips

  • Always check your chain tension
  • Master the art of track standing
  • Practice skid stops in safe areas
  • Keep your ratio street-friendly
  • Remember: brakes are not cheating
