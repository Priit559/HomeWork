About Pedal Faster

Our Story

Est. 2020

Founded in 2020, Pedal Faster has become the leading voice in modern cycling culture. Our magazine brings together passionate cyclists, industry experts, and urban planners to explore the evolving world of two-wheeled transportation.

Our Mission

We believe that bicycles are more than just a mode of transport - they're a catalyst for positive change in our cities and communities. Through in-depth reporting, product reviews, and community stories, we aim to promote cycling as a sustainable, healthy, and exciting lifestyle choice.
