Going Public: A Founder's Journey

March 2, 2025

"We didn't just want to create bikes; we wanted to create a movement that would transform urban transportation."

On a sunny afternoon in the heart of the design district, we sat down with Rob Forbes, the visionary founder of Public Bikes. His journey from design enthusiast to bicycle revolution leader is as fascinating as the bikes his company creates.

The Genesis

After years of traveling through European cities and observing their cycling culture, Forbes became convinced that American cities needed a similar transformation. "I saw how bikes weren't just transportation in Copenhagen and Amsterdam – they were part of the city's soul."
