Cycle Lane Defence: Know Your Rights

Updated: March 2025

This guide provides general information about cyclists' rights. For specific legal advice, please consult a legal professional.

Understanding Your Rights

As cycling continues to grow in popularity, it's crucial to understand your rights and responsibilities on the road. Here's what every cyclist should know.

"Cyclists have the same rights and duties as drivers of motor vehicles, with additional protections in designated cycle lanes."

Key Rights

  • Right to use full lane when necessary
  • Priority in designated cycle lanes
  • Safe passing distance requirement (1.5m minimum)
  • Right to claim damages in accidents
  • Protection under vulnerable road user laws

Common Violations

Most frequent infringements of cyclists' rights:

  • Vehicles parking in cycle lanes
  • Unsafe overtaking
  • Dooring incidents
  • Failure to yield
