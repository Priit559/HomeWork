Fixed Gear Forever

The veloheld combines minimalist design with superb quality. Devoid of excessive graphics and gear shift components, the veloheld product range delights us with its beauty and simplicity. The black and white (ying and yang?) bicycles feature a short wheelbase, a single gear and narrow handlebar... All you need to explore the city streets.

For those who want to create their bikes themselves, the veloheld frames come in three sizes and two colors and are the perfect starting point...

Frame Specifications

  • Columbus Cromor tubing
  • Track geometry
  • Horizontal dropouts
  • Integrated headset ready

Available Sizes

  • Small (52cm)
  • Medium (56cm)
  • Large (59cm)

Color Options

  • Matte Black
  • Pearl White

Quick Facts

Weight: 2.1kg (frame)
BB Standard: BSA
Seat Post: 27.2mm
Fork: 1-1/8" threadless
